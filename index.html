What we do
Charging
Our biggest task is charging the fleet to ensure as many scooters are available and in operation as possible at any given time
Relocation
Collecting up scooters which are outside the permitted operational area is also key to ensuring scooter availbility
Maintenance
Safety of the fleet is important, we want riders to not only enjoy but to also feel safe
Tracking
When a scooter is lifted without a paid ride, we will track, locate and pursue the asset until it is returned
