Who are we

Zoom is one of the first E-Scooter fleet management companies in the UK. We partner with E-Scooter rental companies to bring green electric personal transport to cities. Our fleet engineers travel the UK to maintain and ensure the fleet is safe, charged and ready to scoot.

We are Zoom Scooters.
