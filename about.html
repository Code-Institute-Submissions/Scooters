About Us

Zoom launched in May 2020 during the COVID-19 lockdown due to the UK Government announcing the need for alternative public transport options in particular individual methods where social distancing can be maintained. Zoom partnered with E-Scooter rental companies to bring this proven European model to the UK market.

We are Zoom Scooters.

What we do

Charging

Our biggest task is charging the fleet to ensure at many scooters are available and in operation as possible at any given time

Relocation

Collecting up scooters which are outside the permitted operational area is also key to ensuring scooter availbility

Maintenance

Safety of the fleet is important, we want riders to not only enjoy but to also feel safe

Tracking

When a scooter is lifted without a paid ride, we will track, locate and pursue the asset until it is returned
