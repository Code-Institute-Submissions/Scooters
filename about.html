About Us

Zoom launched in May 2020 during the COVID-19 lockdown due to the UK Government announcing the need for alternative public transport options in particular individual methods where social distancing can be maintained. Zoom partnered with E-Scooter rental companies to bring this proven European model to the UK market.

We are Zoom Scooters.

What we do

Charging

Our biggest task is charging the fleet to ensure at many scooters are available and in operation as possible at any given time. Scooters which need charging alert to the fleet engineers indicating they require charging and show be collect for charging.

Our fleet engineers locate and collect the scooters which require charging and transport them to the local depot for charging. Once charged the scooters are collected and redistributed to the city ready for use.

Scooters last for around 40-80 minutes on a full charge, most rides are much less than this so allow for multiple rides on one full charge.

Relocation

We need to ensure scooters are avialable in the locations they are required. This means our fleet engineers work with the fleet managers to ensure they are placed in locations scooters are most used.

Our fleet engineers locate and collect the scooters which require charging and transport them to the local depot for charging. Once charged the scooters are collected and redistributed to the city ready for use.

Using our vast data from ride history our apps tell the fleet managers and engineers where to place fully charged scooters.

Maintenance

We need to ensure scooters are road ready by repairing any damaged scooters.

We collect the broken scooters and carry out the needed repairs at our depot.

Scooters are hard wearing but often are damaged. We have all the spare parts needed to carry out these repairs without needing to send them back to a factory.

Tracking

Scooter tracking ensures the scooters are only in the authorised areas and not taken inside or away from this area where we operate. It also allows us to stop scooters being stolen.

Scooters that are stolen or removed from the operational area are unavailable for real users to use. This is a loss of income to our business and loss of use to our users.

We monitor all scooters 24/7 and we will react and hunt down scooters when the scooter detects its been illegally moved.
